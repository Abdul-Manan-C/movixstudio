Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our turn-around times, premium delivery formats, and cinematic editing workflows.

Our standard delivery for high-end cinematic edits ranges between 5 to 7 business days depending on complexity. If you have an urgent campaign, broadcast, or launch deadline, we offer an Express Delivery upgrade that guarantees pristine production-ready exports within 48 hours.

We ingest and work with all major industry-standard formats, including ProRes, RED RAW, Blackmagic RAW, ARRI RAW, MP4, and MOV. Footage can be shot in any resolution up to 8K. You can deliver files to us via our secured, encrypted workspace links, or share via Frame.io, Dropbox, Google Drive, or NAS storage.

Our process is engineered to ensure seamless collaboration in 4 elite phases:

1. Strategy & Intake: We analyze your target demographic, branding cues, and pacing requirements.
2. Cinematic Craft: Our master editors build the timeline, craft pristine soundscapes, apply custom typography, and perform custom color grading.
3. Refinement Loop: We share a review link on Frame.io for your frame-accurate feedback.
4. Premium Delivery: We export and deliver the final master package along with optimized alternate aspect ratios for omni-channel distribution.

We use professional-grade video collaboration portals (such as Frame.io) that let you leave timestamped, frame-accurate comments and visual drawings directly on our video draft. All projects include dedicated revisions loops to guarantee that the final export perfectly translates your exact aesthetic vision.
